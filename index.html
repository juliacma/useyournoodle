Appetizers

Mains

Cacio e Pepe

Literally "cheese and pepper", this minimalist cacio e pepe is like a stripped-down mac and cheese.

Thai Peanut Chicken Ramen

All made in one pot, using healthy, pantry staple, ingredients. It's creamy, spicy, filled with veggies, lean chicken, and ramen noodles.

Adam's Mac n' Cheese Chili

Adam invented this over the summer because he indulges himself.

Desserts

Noodle Kugel

Kugel is a baked noodle pudding. It's a traditional Ashkenazi Jewish dish, often served on Shabbat and Yom Tov.

Falooda

Falooda is a cold dessert with origins in the Indian subcontinent. Traditionally it is made from mixing rose syrup, vermicelli, sweet basil seeds with milk, often served with ice cream.
