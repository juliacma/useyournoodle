Adam's Mac n' Cheese Chili

Ingredients:

1 box of mac n' cheese (plus milk, butter)

can of beans, black or red (precooked ones, unless you know how to cook beans)

lots of seasoning

ground turkey

1 onion

1 pepper

some good hot sauce

oil

maybe a tomato

Directions:

  1. Follow directions on box to make Mac n' Cheese. Make it.

    2. While you’re doing that:

  2. Cut up some onions, throw them in a pan on low heat with some oil
  3. Put ground turkey in and season it with some spices, be fun

    4. 3.5. Throw little pieces of pepper into the pan at some point. Also some hot sauce if you want extra credit.

  4. Once the turkey is almost done cooking, add in your beans. Pre-made beans come with some juice, but if there isn’t enough for your desired texture, add in some tomato sauce if you have it or some tomato.
  5. Season the turkey bean mixture more.
  6. Be done with the mac and cheese around now.
  7. Serve in a bowl with mac and cheese on bottom, chili on top, hot sauce dressed.

    8. yum
